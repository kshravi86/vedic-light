Privacy Policy

Vedic Light respects your privacy. The app performs Vedic astrology calculations on‑device and does not require an account. Birth details entered in the app remain on your device unless you explicitly share them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data is not transmitted to our servers.
